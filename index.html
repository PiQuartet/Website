Pi Quartet

Consisting of Leah Plave (cello), Oksana Mukosii (viola), Leslee Smucker (violin), and Hoei Lien The (violin), this half-American, half-Dutch and Ukrainian string quartet is dedicated to experimentation and exploring the infinite possibilities of this combination of string instruments. The idea to form a quartet emerged from a shared interest in multidisciplinary performances and curiosity about music that lies outside the traditional string quartet canon.
